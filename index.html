ABOUT THE IOT PROJECT

The Internet of Transport (IoT) aims to provide a toolkit for analysts, data scientists, modellers and developers from the public and private sectors in the transport and mobility industry to:
(1) gather data from sources on the web,
(2) store it in the cloud, and
(3) produce insights through visualisation, analytics, alerts and messaging etc.
